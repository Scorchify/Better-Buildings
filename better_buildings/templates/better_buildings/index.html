About MCPS BB

MCPS Better Buildings is a passion project created by  Ayden Yeung   and  Julian Givens  of Montgomery County Public Schools. The project is a response complaints from students about building conditions at a number of middle and high schools in the county.

MCPS BB is designed to allow for direct communication between students and their building service teams, as well as to administrators in the central office. We hope this has the effect of speeding up the repair process, ensuring a safe and enjoyable environment for MCPS students to learn in. If you're a student interested in bringing this to your school, reach out to us!
